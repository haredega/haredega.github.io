Hi, ever seen/heard about Scouts? You might think it's something for kids. It kinda is. But then, it is so much more.
It is a real school for life, where kids and adults develop themselves based strongly on the *Learn by doing* motto.

Why am I even talking about it? Well, since 2013, I've been a Scout Leader.
As you read, I might be preparing our next activity. As a team of 5 cub scout chiefs, we work with about 30 cub-scouts. We propose activities that help them discover the nature and develop themselves around 6 axes of personal growth.

It is very gratifying to work within such an association, that not only is acknowledged at an international level, but where values are in the heart of everything we do.
As a Scout, I really believe that teaching by the example and supporting empowerment of the youth are the keys for a better world.
The more our children know know their place in society and the impacts of their decisions, the sooner we will have a world where citizens reach out for peace and understanding throughout different cultures, and the more we'll have initiatives to try and minimize environmental impacts.

As I'm living in France, I decided to participate with the [Scouts et Guides de France](https://www.sgdf.fr/), in a local group called [Sevre et Loire](https://blogs.sgdf.fr/selo/)
Before that, I was also a volunteer at [Grupo Escoteiro Utu-Guaçú](http://www.utuguacu.org.br/) until june 2017. Earlier that year, many of us got a condecoration for the years we had been working for the Scout Movement. That makes us want to be even better guides to the children we help develop themselves.
Want to know more? Take a look at [Scouts.org](https://www.scout.org) or, if you are a scout, on [my profile](https://www.scout.org/user/114091)
