I thought the first post I'd be making should be about the reasons that make me create yet another blog. So, here are some of them:

A 1st shot at maintaining a real-life web-page
===
It's been more than a year since I started my very first e-learning course on web technologies on  [Udacity](https://www.udacity.com/course/intro-to-html-and-css--ud304). Even though so far I've been able to complete all the projects on the courses I've done, I never really did something with all of that. So, my first reason is to get real with my new skills.
*This means that hopefully I'll be trying new stuff here from time to time*

A place to expose my career doubts, reflections and experiences
===
Right now I'm starting out a career, which means I have a lot of questions and very few answers. I thought it could be a good start to share some of them and see what happens. If you have something to say or any ideas of what I can write about, feel free to contact me on twitter!

Getting back to writing
===
In the past I used to love writing, but juggling between an engineering course and a full-time internship hasn't helped me out with that life balance thing.
As I'll be finishing college in a little less than a month, it is about time to start to check some items of that *"Things I'll do after I graduate"* list, which includes writing from time to time.

Finally,
Why using [Minimal Mistakes](https://mmistakes.github.io/minimal-mistakes/)?
===
I think it might be nice to explain why I'm using a theme instead of something I made up from scratch. Well, as I'm not yet 100% comfortable with my own design choices - being an engineer, it is quite easy to get it all wrong - for now I'm sticking to this simple yet well designed solution.
But as I said, my intention is to play around with this website, so who knows when will it be all of my work?
