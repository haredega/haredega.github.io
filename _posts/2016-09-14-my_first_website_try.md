My 1st website tryout
===

What I actually did though was finally staring my first website project. It is
not yet on-line, but it is already 'Waiting for Approval'. This first attempt
ended up being something for my father, as a sort of gift.

He works in a clinic, and since I gave him a domain as his last year's Christmas
gift, I've been pushing him to contact and ask for a professional web designer
to do it. After a crazy semester for me, I noticed he was never gonna do it.
I decided then to do it myself - not before warning him a gazillion times that
I'm not the best choice because even if I can code, I got no idea on how to do
good SEO or how to get a design with a 100% fluid UX. That beins said, I started
out with the same model I used for the "error 404" page I put online as soon as
I got him the domain.

(https://s3-sa-east-1.amazonaws.com/haredega-git-imgs/error404.PNG)

I wanted to keep it simple, so I decided to let only the essential information -
so I have a 1 level taxonomy with only 5 static web pages.

(https://s3-sa-east-1.amazonaws.com/haredega-git-imgs/header.PNG)

As I also wanted to showcase the people working there and the place itself, I
made two separate pages for that. Same to the treatment options. As is is a
Chinese Medicine clinic, I put that on top of the treatment options,
along with a brief explanation of how it works.

When I get final approval from all of the doctors, I'm gonna host it at AWS
with, at first, a free tier account - in a bucket where the error page already
is. *I'll post the link as soon as it's available.*
